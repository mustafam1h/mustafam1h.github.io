Empowering Children with Special Needs

Providing specialized educational and rehabilitation programs to support and integrate children with special needs into society.

Why we stand out

Specialized Care and Support

At reda Salama Foundation, we offer specialized care and support for children with special needs. Our experienced professionals provide individual and group therapy sessions in various areas such as psychological therapy, occupational therapy, and physical therapy. We are dedicated to enhancing their abilities and helping them integrate into society.

Tailored Educational Programs

We provide tailored educational programs that cater to the unique needs of each child. Our programs are designed to promote their intellectual, emotional, and social development. With our specialized approach, we ensure that every child receives the necessary skills and knowledge to reach their full potential.

A Beacon of Hope

RedaSalama Foundation is a beacon of hope for children with special needs and their families. We create a nurturing and supportive environment where children can thrive and grow. Our mission is to empower these children and help them overcome challenges, fostering a sense of belonging and inclusion in the community.

Services

Check out our service offerings

Individual Therapy Sessions

Our individual therapy sessions are designed to meet the unique needs of each child. Our experienced therapists work closely with the child to address their specific challenges and develop strategies to overcome them. Through a combination of evidence-based techniques and personalized interventions, we aim to enhance the child's communication, social, and emotional skills, and improve their overall well-being.

Contact us

Group Therapy Sessions

Our group therapy sessions provide a supportive and inclusive environment for children to interact with their peers. Led by our skilled therapists, these sessions focus on promoting social skills, cooperation, and teamwork. Through engaging activities and exercises, we help children build positive relationships, improve their communication abilities, and develop a sense of belonging and acceptance within the group.

Contact us

Parent Education and Support

We understand that parents play a crucial role in the growth and development of their children. That's why we offer parent education and support programs to empower parents with the knowledge and skills they need to support their child's progress. Our workshops and individual consultations provide guidance on various topics, such as behavior management, communication strategies, and navigating the special education system. We also offer a safe and understanding space for parents to share their experiences, concerns, and successes, fostering a supportive community.

Our Company

Reda Salama Foundation is an organization that aims to provide support and care for children with special needs. The foundation was established in response to the urgent need for a specialized educational and rehabilitation environment for these children, seeking to enhance their abilities and help them integrate into society. The foundation serves as a beacon of hope, offering a variety of programs and services including individual and group rehabilitation sessions supervised by experienced specialists in multiple fields such as psychotherapy, occupational therapy, and physical therapy.
